Overview

The Office of the Vice-President for Planning and Finance (OVPPF) is primarily responsible in financial planning, management and operations of the University.

In line with the budget and planning operations, the OVPPF-supervised UP System Budget Office provides financial and financial-related reports of the University. Data are consolidated annually for submission to the concerned agencies such as the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), the Department of Budget and Management (DBM), the House of Representatives and the Senate. These data serve as references for the UP Budget Proposals and technical budget hearings.

It is imperative therefore, that effective data collection management be designed to ensure prompt compliance to government's requirements and to provide quality and reliable consolidated reports of the University.
